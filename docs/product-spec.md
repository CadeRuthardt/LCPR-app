

# Product Specification

# Le Chateau Pet Resort Mobile App

Version: 1.0
Status: Living Document

---

# Executive Summary

The Le Chateau Mobile App is the primary digital experience for Le Chateau Pet Resort clients.

Its purpose is to extend the luxury hospitality experience beyond the physical resort while allowing Gingr to remain the operational system of record.

The application is intentionally not a kennel management system.

Clients should feel like they are interacting with Le Chateau—not Gingr.

---

# Product Vision

Create the most premium digital experience in the pet hospitality industry.

The app should feel comparable to leading luxury travel and hospitality applications while simplifying every interaction a guest has with Le Chateau.

The application should continue providing value even when a guest does not have an active stay.

---

# Product Goals

- Strengthen the Le Chateau brand.
- Replace the generic Gingr client portal experience.
- Reduce friction during the reservation request process.
- Provide transparency throughout every stay.
- Increase client engagement between stays.
- Simplify communication without creating additional work for staff.
- Build a platform that can evolve independently of Gingr.

---

# Target Users

## Primary

Current Le Chateau clients.

## Secondary

Prospective clients researching the resort.

## Internal

Reception staff interact only when processing reservation requests or operational workflows.

The client app should never become an employee application.

---

# Product Principles

1. Hospitality before technology.
2. Luxury before feature quantity.
3. Gingr remains the source of truth.
4. Every feature should reduce friction.
5. Clients should never see internal operational terminology.
6. The experience should be consistent across iPhone and Android.

# Reservation Experience

Clients submit reservation requests, not confirmed reservations.

Reception reviews requests and manually creates reservations in Gingr.

The client experience should resemble requesting a reservation at a luxury resort rather than filling out kennel software forms.

Clients select an experience:

- Classic
- Premium
- VIP
- Let Le Chateau Recommend

Reception assigns the actual accommodation.

# Current Stay Experience

During an active stay, the app becomes a live companion.

Features may include:

- Stay timeline
- Daily updates
- Photos
- Live cameras
- Pickup information
- Contact resort

When no active stay exists, these features disappear naturally.

---

# Communication Strategy

Communication should be channel-agnostic.

Reception performs one action.

The backend determines whether the client receives:

- Push notification
- SMS
- Email

The app acts as the long-term archive for stay updates.
